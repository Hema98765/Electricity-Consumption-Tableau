Project Overview

This project analyzes electricity consumption patterns using Tableau. It studies time-of-day usage, seasonal variations, and sector-wise electricity consumption.

Objectives

  • Analyze electricity usage at different times of the day
  • Identify peak demand periods
  • Study seasonal variations
  • Compare sector-wise consumption

Key Insights

  • Evening hours show peak residential usage
  • Summer season has highest electricity demand
  • Industrial sector consumes the most power
